About the Book

Dear Little Me is a powerful guide for anyone who ever felt \"too much\" or \"not enough.\" Patrycja Marta Jerushalmy shares her raw, unfiltered journey of embracing ADHD and unlocking the gifts of authenticity, resilience, and creativity. This is not just a story — it’s a roadmap to living free, original, and fully yourself.

What You'll Discover

  • How ADHD can be a superpower, not a flaw
  • Strategies to overcome perfectionism and self-doubt
  • Tools for thriving with authenticity and self-love
  • Real pathways to personal growth and emotional resilience

About the Author

Patrycja Marta Jerushalmy is a passionate writer, entrepreneur, and advocate for authenticity, self-love, and personal growth. Through her bold journey of embracing ADHD, she empowers others to transform what once felt like \"too much\" into a source of strength, creativity, and freedom. Her mission is simple: to remind every soul that they are — and have always been — enough.
